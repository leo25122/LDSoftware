Distributed Architecture

Forget isolated Excel sheets. Forge relies on a powerful and scalable client/server architecture designed for the most demanding environments.

  • Arsenal Server: Proprietary Forge database on Raspberry Pi (no third-party software) synchronized in real-time via SignalR.
  • MainApp: High-end desktop client. An ultra-fast interface designed to master your plant's complexity, saving hours of development and eliminating error margins.
  • Team Sync: Work collaboratively on the same plant with peace of mind. The system coordinates access in real-time, ensuring a seamless workflow and eliminating the risk of data loss.

Universal Export

Generate the required code directly for your preferred Target. Full support for top-tier industrial ecosystems.

  • Target PLC: Native compatibility with Siemens S7-1500, S7-400, and Allen-Bradley ControlLogix.
  • Target HMI: Immediate generation of tags and structures for WinCC Classic/Unified, Ignition, and iFix.
  • Massive Auto-Generation: A true arsenal of integrated functions that go well beyond I/O Marshalling, automating the creation of logic, alarms, and entire configurations with a single click.

TIA Openness Integration

Seamlessly integrate with Siemens TIA Portal through powerful Openness API, enabling direct code generation and management within your engineering environment.

  • Direct TIA Portal Integration: Write code directly into TIA Portal projects without manual imports or conversions, streamlining your entire automation workflow.
  • Automated Code Management: Automatically generate, update, and synchronize PLC blocks, data types, and symbolic information within your TIA environment.
  • Seamless Engineering Flow: Eliminate context switching between tools. Design your plant logic in Forge and instantly apply it to your TIA Portal projects with full traceability and version control.

Security & Licensing

Enterprise-grade cryptographic protection for maximum intellectual property security.

  • RSA-4096 & DPAPI: Advanced authentication and cryptography natively integrated into the Windows environment.
  • Hardware Fingerprint: 5-point unique fingerprint generation to prevent unauthorized access or copying on automation servers.
