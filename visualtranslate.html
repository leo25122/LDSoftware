Tesseract OCR Technology

Instantly translate any visible element on your monitor, eliminating the need to copy-paste text or switch between applications.

  • Recognition Engine: Leverages the reliability of the Tesseract OCR w64 engine for extremely accurate character reading.
  • Dynamic Overlay: Define the capture area with a simple mouse drag, and the system will apply the overlay translation directly on software, games, or locked interfaces.

100% Offline Translation

Download the architecture once, use it anywhere, ensuring total privacy for your on-screen data.

  • Argos Translate Engine: Access a vast catalog of language packages locally downloaded for on-device processing.
  • Absolute Privacy: No text or image is ever sent to external Cloud APIs. All neural processing happens directly on your machine's CPU.

Standalone Execution

Architecture designed to be lightweight and non-invasive.

  • Modular Installation: Requires the initial installation of Tesseract (Windows 64-bit), then integrates seamlessly with the system.
  • Portable Executable: After the initial setup, the entire Python application can be moved and run as a single independent file.
